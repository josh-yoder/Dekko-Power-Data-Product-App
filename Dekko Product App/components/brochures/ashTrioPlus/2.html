AMPLIFIED POWER
A pinnacle of power capacity, the Ashley Trio features three outlets that
deliver AC power for high-power devices, as well two innovative USB ports.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip to monitor and
independently deliver the required amperage to your devices — up to 2.1
amps each — and charge them in half the time it takes with similar products
on the market.
The Ashley Trio’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2, FCC Part 15A
approved, as well as, meets USB.org Battery Charging 1.2 speciﬁcations.
