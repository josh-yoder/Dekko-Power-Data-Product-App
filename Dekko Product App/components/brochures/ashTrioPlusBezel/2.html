POWER TO THE PEOPLE
Bringing extended capabilities and uninterrupted connection away from
the traditional worksurfaces, the multi-user-friendly Trio Bezel includes
three AC outlets and a state-of-the-art dual USB port.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip to monitor and
independently deliver the required amperage to your devices — up to
2.1 amps each — and charge them in half the time it takes with similar
products on the market.
The Ashley Trio Bezel’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2, FCC
Part 15A approved, as well as, meets UL spill requirements and USB.org
Battery Charging 1.2 speciﬁcations.
MAKE YOUR MARK
The Ashley Trio Bezel can impress like any other smart accessory.
Both the durable metal bezel and plastic face are available in
classic White, Gray, and Black, while the fresh plastic tints
include: Neon Red, Harvest Pumpkin, Blue Daisy, and Light Green.
More custom plastic and/or color painted metal trim are available
as a special order.
The frame allows mounting where it makes the most impact —
embedded in furniture or on a vertical or horizontal surface.
