TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
Work Space Power, USB Charging, and Data Solutions
Dekko power and charging products respond to your
needs —where you need power and communication most.
Dekko products are designed to meet market demand for
power and fast charging devices with elegant and reﬁned
designs, as well as, state-of-the-art technology interface
and fast USB charging capabilities.
USB Charging
The Ashley and Staﬀord lines feature USB charging ports
with 2.1 Amps per port, 4.2 Amps total with a smart device
recognition chip that monitors the power request from a
device to self-regulate and supply all 2.1 Amps per port, or
less, as needed by device.
The result is power hungry devices won’t steal power away
from smaller devices and each device charges as fast as
possible, getting the power each device needs.
Ashley Power and Data Ports
Ashley models feature dual USB ports, two to four simplexes
and options for a choice of HDMI, RJ45 or VGA ports, in
clamp on, under surface mount, and recessed versions.
All are available as stand-alone furniture power distribution
units, with a NEMA 5-15 plug, conduit, or whip connectors.
Please contact your Dekko salesperson for information
about conduit options.
Staﬀord Power and Data Ports
Staﬀord and Staﬀord Mini conference solutions feature
options for two to four each of: AC outlets, Extron
“Mini” MAAP adapter plates for customized additions and
conﬁgurations, USB 2.1 Amp ports with smart recognition
chips, and a cable storage cavity.
Staﬀord models are
available as a stand-alone furniture power distribution unit,
with a NEMA 5-15 plug,
conduit, or whip connectors.
Please contact your Dekko salesperson for information
about conduit options.
Safety Requirements for Electrical
Dekko Ashley and Staﬀord AC receptacles meet UL spill
requirements, USB ports are UL 1310 recognized, Class 2,
FCC Part 15A approved, and meet USB.org Battery Charging
1.2 speciﬁcations. The Low Voltage System (LVS) is FCC Part
15 B approved for residential and contract use and a Listed UL
1310 Class 2 power supply system.
Small Footprint Requirements
The Mini Adapt, with a half-moon design and clamp on
small foot print, features two asymmetrical receptacles
to accommodate all types of plugs at the same time and is
available as a relocatable power unit, with a NEMA 5-15 plug.
LVS: Award Winning Technology
Dekko’s 2016 Best of NeoCon Gold and Silver Award-winning
Low Voltage System, with 2.4 Amps per USB port, has the
fastest charging capability in the industry.
LVS is designed
with intelligence to interact with up to 16 devices to provide
all the power each needs and know when to shutdown to
protect the USB modules from incorrect installation.
Developed with an eye to the future as tablets, laptops,
and other devices in contract and residential environments
convert to USB charging, LVS can be a discrete, ﬂexible,
retroﬁt plug-and-play solution, or a custom solution, oﬀering
degrees of design freedom either way.
