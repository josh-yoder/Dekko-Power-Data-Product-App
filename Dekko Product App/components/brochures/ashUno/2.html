DISCREET POWER & USB CHARGING
The Ashley Uno Series provides easily accessible power
in a small, discreet package. The AC outlet and USB ports
are integrated within a single piece cover, resulting in
fewer edge lines and a clean aesthetic.
POWER PERFECTION
Ashley Uno, Uno Under, and Uno Bezel feature one AC
outlet for high-power devices and dual 2.1 amp USB
charging ports.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device
Recognition Chip to monitor and independently deliver
the required amperage to your devices — up to 2.1 amps
each — and charge them in half the time it takes with
similar products on the market.
HIGH STANDARDS
The Ashley Uno Series is both UL962A and UL111 listed.
