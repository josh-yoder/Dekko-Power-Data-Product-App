SMOOTH, CLEAN FINISH
The Ashley 210 Series expands the Ashley Duo line by
creating a fresh, modern look with a smooth ﬁnish. With the
top metal bracket removed, the full beauty of the design
comes to life, allowing you to incorporate the Ashley 210
into your space as a dramatic focal point. The 210 meets
power demands while providing beautiful integration
into your space.
POWER PERFECTION
The Ashley 210 and 210 Under feature two AC outlets
for high-power devices and dual 2.1 amp USB charging
ports. Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition
Chip to monitor and independently deliver the required
amperage to your devices — up to 2.1 amps each — and
charge them in half the time it takes with similar products
on the market.
HIGH STANDARDS
The Ashley 210 Series is both UL 962A and UL 111 listed.
