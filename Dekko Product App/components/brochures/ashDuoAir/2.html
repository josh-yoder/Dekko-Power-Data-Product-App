PENT-UP POWER
The Ashley Duo Air combines steadfast power—available at the slightest
touch — with hideaway capability to make the most of your worksurfaces
and furniture.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip to monitor and
independently deliver the required amperage to your devices — up to
2.1 amps each — and charge them in half the time it takes with similar
products on the market.
The Ashley Duo Air’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2, FCC
Part 15A approved, as well as, meets UL spill requirements and USB.org
Battery Charging 1.2 speciﬁcations.
CLEVER AND QUIET CONFIDENCE
The intelligent design behind the Ashley Duo Air yields a trim,
attractive cover that pops open to reveal an integrated wedge
of AC outlets and USB ports. Equipped with a cushioned, gas
strut mechanism for discreet and dependable use, this Ashley
Duo version opens and closes quiet as “air.”
The metal bezel, cover and plastic face are available in classic
White, Gray, and Black, while the fresh plastic tints include:
Neon Red, Harvest Pumpkin, Blue Daisy, and Light Green.
More custom plastic and/or color painted metal trim are available
as a special order.
