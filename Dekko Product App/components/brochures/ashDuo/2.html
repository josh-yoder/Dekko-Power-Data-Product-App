ELITE POWER WITHIN
Inside the attractive casing resides a most impressive power pack.
Two outlets promise reliable AC power for plugged-in hardware, while
the true star of the tandem is the dual 2.1 amp USB port.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip to monitor
and independently deliver the required amperage to your devices —up
to 2.1 amps each — and charge them in half the time it takes with similar
products on the market.
The Ashley Duo’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2, FCC Part
15A approved, as well as, meets USB.org Battery Charging 1.2 speciﬁcations.

EASE OF INSTALLATION
With a straightforward, thin mounting bracket,
the Ashley Duo installation is a snap. Slide the
bracket behind the worksurface — it will even
slip in between a panel and the desktop — clip
in the bottom half from underneath, and tight-
en the thumb screws.
The simplicity enhances your capability to
relocate the Ashley Duo on your worksurface.
Add the mounting clamps to the underside of
the surface to keep the power cord tucked out
of sight, safe and secure.
