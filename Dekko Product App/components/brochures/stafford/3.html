FEATURES
The Staﬀord features four electrical AC 15 Amp power outlets, four
USB ports, and four blank Extron “Mini” MAAP (Mini Architectural
Adapter Plates) plates. Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device
Recognition Chip to monitor and independently deliver the required
amperage to your devices—up to 2.1 amps each—and charge them
in half the time it takes with similar products on the market.
Staﬀord USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2, FCC Part 15A
approved, and meets USB.org Battery Charging 1.2 speciﬁcations
and UL Spill Resistance requirements.
Standard cord options are: 10-foot Black PVC Free NEMA Cord,
1-foot Black PVC Free Perimeter Cord, or 8-foot Conduit (for
hardwire connection).
Standard Finishes:
• Flange and doors oﬀered in Satin Silver,
Satin Black and Satin White
• Recessed cavity is Satin Black
• Receptacles and USB ports are Black Plastic.
Extron plates are black metal
• NEMA and Perimeter cords are Black
• Conduit is Silver
• Length: 18.5”
Width: 5”
