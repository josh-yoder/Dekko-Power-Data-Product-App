EASY ACCESS TO POWER
In it’s Under iteration, the Ashley Duo provides easily accessible power
in a more discreet package. Two outlets relay reliable AC power for
plugged-in hardware, while the dual 2.1 amp USB port steals the show
with its impressive capability.
Each USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip to monitor and
independently deliver the required amperage to your devices — up to
2.1 amps each — and charge them in half the time it takes with similar
products on the market.
The Ashley Duo Under’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2,
FCC Part 15A approved, as well as, meets USB.org Battery Charging
1.2 speciﬁcations.
DISTINCT STYLE, OUT OF THE WAY
For those who prefer behind-the-scenes charging, the Ashley Duo
Under oﬀers an array of ﬁnishes reﬂecting subtle, monochromatic
elegance to attention-grabbing custom colors.
The Ashley Duo Under’s featured power combination is completely
integrated within a single-piece cover, eliminating dust and debris
catching grooves and seams.
The slim-reveal metal mounting plate and plastic face are available
in classic White, Gray, and Black, while the fresh plastic tints include:
