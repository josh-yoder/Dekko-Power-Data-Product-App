BEST-IN-CLASS POWER COMBINATION
The impressive Ashley Trio Under features three outlets that deliver
AC power for high-power devices, as well as one of the fastest-
charging USB ports on the market.
The dual 2.1 amp USB port utilizes a Smart Device Recognition Chip
to monitor and independently deliver the required amperage to your
devices — up to 2.1 amps each —and charge them in half the time it
takes with similar products on the market.
The Ashley Trio Under’s USB charging is UL 1310 recognized, Class 2,
FCC Part 15A approved, as well as, meets USB.org Battery Charging
1.2 speciﬁcations.
GO FOR AN UNDER-STATED LOOK
The Ashley Trio Under can complement your brand
with unobtrusive shades or brash blushes.
The slim-reveal metal mounting plate and plastic face
are available in classic White, Gray, and Black, while
the fresh plastic tints include: Neon Red, Harvest
Pumpkin, Blue Daisy, and Light Green.
More custom plastic and/or color painted metal trim
are available as a special order.
The Ashley Trio Under’s outlets and USB ports are
integrated within a single-piece cover, resulting in
fewer edges and a cleaner aesthetic.
CHOICE DATA OPTIONS
Outﬁtting the Trio Under with your choice of
an HDMI, RJ45 or VGA port ensures all your data
